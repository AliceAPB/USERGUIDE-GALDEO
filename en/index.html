User Guide

Welcome to Galdeo

Everything you need to get started and master Galdeo, step by step.

Table of Contents

STEP 1 — Connect

Access your Galdeo workspace securely.

1

Go to the login page

Visit app.galdeo.com/login or click "Log in" from the website.

2

Enter your credentials

Type your email and password, then click "Sign in".

3

Forgot your password?

Click "Forgot password" and follow the instructions sent by email.

Security: Never share your login credentials. Enable two-factor authentication in your security settings.

STEP 2 — Set up your profile

Personalise your space and fill in your professional information.

1

Open your settings

Click your avatar in the top right and select "Settings".

2

Complete your profile

Add your photo, name, agency name, and professional contact details.

3

Set your preferences

Choose your currency, interface language, and notification preferences.

Tip: A complete profile builds client trust when sending quotes.

STEP 3 — Create a travel quote

Build and send a professional travel quote in just a few clicks.

1

Click "New quote"

From the dashboard, click "+ New quote" in the top right.

2

Fill in the trip details

Enter the destination, dates, number of travellers and type of service.

3

Add services

Select accommodation, transport and activities from your supplier catalogue.

4

Send the quote

Preview your quote and send it directly to the client by email.

STEP 4 — Edit your quote

Adjust, correct and update your quote at any time.

1

Open the existing quote

From your quotes list, click on the one you want to edit.

2

Edit the services

Add, remove or rearrange items in the quote based on client feedback.

3

Save and resend

Save your changes and send the new version to the client with an explanatory note.

Tip: Every version of your quote is saved automatically. You can view the change history at any time.

STEP 5 — Manage your projects

Track all your trips and client files in one place.

1

View your projects

Go to Projects in the sidebar menu to see all your active files.

2

Track statuses

Each project shows its status: quote sent, pending, confirmed or closed.

3

Collaborate with your team

Assign team members to each project and share information in real time.

OPTIONAL — Add your suppliers

Build your supplier catalogue to compose quotes faster.

1

Go to "Suppliers"

In the sidebar, click Suppliers then "+ Add".

2

Fill in the details

Enter the name, category (hotel, transport, activity…), country and rates.

3

Save and use

Your supplier is immediately available in the quote creation form.

Tip: Organise your suppliers by category and destination to quickly find the right services.

OPTIONAL — Import your suppliers

Bulk import your existing catalogue via a CSV or Excel file.

1

Prepare your file

Download the CSV template provided by Galdeo and fill it with your supplier data.

2

Start the import

In Suppliers → Import, drop your file or click to select it.

3

Review and confirm

Galdeo shows a preview of the imported data. Check the information then click "Confirm".

Warning: Import overwrites detected duplicates. Back up your existing data before running a bulk import.
